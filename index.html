About

In Honor of Connor


On October 15th, 2014, the University of Virginia lost one of its own in Connor Cormier. A brilliant student by all accounts, Connor studied Computer Science and loved programming competitions in all forms. So to honor and remember him, we’re doing just that: bringing students together to Charlottesville to code to their hearts’ delights. . .

C4K

. . .And when we’re through, we’ll help the next generation of hackers follow in Connor’s footsteps by donating proceeds to Computers4Kids. C4K strives “to improve low income youth’s computer and learning skills through caring mentorship, structured training, a vibrant learning environment, and access to a computer at home.”
